Romberg’s Test:
  1. Explain the test procedure and give proper instruction to the subject.
  2. The subject is asked to stand up with his feet together, once with their eyes open and then with their eyes closed.
  3. Subject with sensory ataxia will sway or may fall even when their eyes are closed but they will stand normally with the eyes open.
  4. Subject with cerebellar ataxia will sway to both sides, whether their eyes are open or closed (Hutchison’s).
