Procedure:
  1. For recording the ECG, the following steps are followed.
  2. The individual is requested to remove any metallic items on the body.
  3. He is made to lie down bare chest comfortably in supine position on a bed.
  4. The ECG jelly is applied to the limb leads RA, LA, LL and RL.
  5. The limb leads marked RA and LA are placed snugly over the right wrist and left wrist region respectively.
  6. Apply jelly to the chest leads and fix them in the following anatomical positions:
  • i. V1 is placed in the fourth intercostal space just to the right of the sternum.
  • ii. V2 is placed in the fourth intercostal space
