Accomodation reflex:
  1. Explain the test procedure to the subject
  2. Sit opposite a bright light source and hold one finger close to subject’s nose
  3. Ask the subject to look at distant object
  4. Ask the subject to look suddenly at his finger
  5. Repeat the whole procedure with the subject’s other eye as well
