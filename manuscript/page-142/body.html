Electrodes:

The standard leads that are used for recording the ECG are unipolar and bipolar leads. In a unipolar lead, one of the electrodes is the active electrode or the recording electrode and the other is the reference or indifferent electrode. In contrast, in bipolar leads, both electrodes are active.
