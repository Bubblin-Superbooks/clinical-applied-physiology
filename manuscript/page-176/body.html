Case no. 2

Malabsorption Syndrome

A 43-year-old man comes to the clinic complaining of abdominal cramps. The patient has a 15-year history of alcohol abuse. He has been complaining about passing pale and malodorous stools that are difficult to flush over the past 6 months.


Questions:
  1. What is your diagnosis?
  2. What is Malabsorption Syndrome?
  3. What are its causes?
  4. What are its symptoms?
Answers:
  1. Malabsorption Syndrome.
