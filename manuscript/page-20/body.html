Case no. 3:

Poliomyelitis Demyelinatia

A 46 year old man had progressive weakness in all the four limbs, for 8 weeks. He also had difficulty in taking food. His speech is slurred.


Examination of motor system revealed:

  1. Power grade III in all four limbs
  2. Deep reflexes are sluggish
  3. Hypotonia
  4. Twitching of muscle was present
  5. Plantar reflex-flexor bilaterally
