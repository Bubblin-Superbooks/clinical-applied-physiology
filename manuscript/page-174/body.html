  2. Mechanism of acid secretion:

    In the apical cells of gastric parietal cell there exist H+, K+, ATPase that pumps H+ out of the parietal cells in exchange for K. H+ is formed from H2CO3 that is formed by hydration of CO2, catalyzed by carbonic anhydrase. HCO3‐ formed by the dissociation of H2CO3 is extruded out of the basolateral membrane of parietal cells in exchange for Cl‐.

  3. Regulation of acid secretion:
