AXILLARYNODE

From the subject’s front or side, palpate the right axilla with your left hand and viceverse. Gently place your fingertips into the apex of the axilla and then draw them downwards, feeling the medial, anterior and posterior axillary walls in turn.

Keep your nails short to avoid causing (Macleod’s) discomfort.
