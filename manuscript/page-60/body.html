Case no. 4

Hyperthyroidism

A 30 year old woman complained of excessive sweating, increased appetite and loss of weight for past one month. On examination she had tachycardia with high systolic blood pressure.

Her thyroid gland showed diffuse symmetrical enlargement. She had exophthalmos with fine tremors in her outstretched fingers.

Biochemical test showed increased plasma T3 and T4 and slight decrease in plasma TSH level
