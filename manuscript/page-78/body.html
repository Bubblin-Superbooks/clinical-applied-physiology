Cyanosis
  1. Explain the test procedure and make the subject comfortable.
  2. See the bluish discoloration of skin and or mucous membrane.
  3. It is commonly seen at sites were skin is thin for e.g. mucous membrane of undersurface of tongue, lips, earlobes, nail beds and tip of nose.
  4. For central cyanosis look at lips and tongue.
  5. For peripheral cyanosis look at hands , ears or feets.
