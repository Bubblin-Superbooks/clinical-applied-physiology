Case no. 3

Myxoedema

A 45 year old woman presented with the complaints of easy fatigability and lack of appetite for past one month. On examination it was found that her skin is dry, thickened, and yellowish with a puffy face and course hair.

She also complained of recent loss of her eyebrow. Her voice was husky. Neurological examination showed delayed reflexes. Biochemical examination showed high blood cholesterol level.
