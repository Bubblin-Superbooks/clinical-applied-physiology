1. Look across the neck from the right of the patient
  1. Identify the jugular vein pulsations
  2. If not certain use the Abdomino-jugular reflex or occlusion to confirm it is the JVP
  3. The JVP is the vertical height in centimeters between the top of the venous pulsations and the sternal angle
  4. Identify the timing and form of pulsation and note any abnormality
