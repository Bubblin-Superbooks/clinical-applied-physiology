Rebound phenomena:
  1. Seat the subject on a chair comfortably.
  2. Explain the test procedure.
  3. Ask the subject to stretch his arm out in front and maintain this position(Macleod’s)
  4. Push the subject wrist quickly downward and observe the returning(Macleod’s)
Romberg’s test:
  1. Explain the test procedure and give proper instruction
  2. The subject is asked to stand up with his feet together, once with their eyes open and then with their eyes closed.
  3. Subjects with sensory ataxia will sway/even fall when eyes are closed but will stand normally with eyes open.
