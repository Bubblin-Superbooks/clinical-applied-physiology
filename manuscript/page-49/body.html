  3. Which hormones are affected in this patient?

Answer

  1. Anterior pituitary
  2. Thyroid gland, adrenal cortex, ovaries
    1. Gonadotropic hormones (FSH, LH)-deficiency causes amenorrhea
    2. Lack of TSH- hence patient becomes tired, loss of weight is observed, cannot tolerate cold weather, decrease in blood pressure and slow pulse rate
    3. Lack of ACTH-there is intolerance of stress
