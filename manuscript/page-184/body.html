Question:

Calculate the E.C.F. volume and its percentage (%) of T.B.W. from the following data:

1. The Amount of Thiosulphate Injected: 590 mg.
2. Plasma concentration at the end of 1 hour: 3 mg/100 cc.
3. Amount of substance excreted in 1 hour: 20 mg.

Assume 45 liters of T.B.W. as 100%.

Answer:

Using the formula:

Conc. = Amount รท Volume,
Amount = Injected - Excreted.

Substituting for values from the table above: 

Volume of E.C.F   =   (590-20)/0.03      
                                    = 19000mL or 19L.      
Percentage of T.B.W = 19/45 * 100                      
    = 42%.
