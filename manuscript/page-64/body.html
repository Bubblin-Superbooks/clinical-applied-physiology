Case no. 7

Diabetes Mellitus (Type I)

A 50 year old man presented with complaints of polyuria, polydipsia and loss of weight in spite of increased appetite for past two months.

Biochemical examination showed high fasting and postprandial blood sugar level.

Questions:
  1. What is the probable diagnosis?
  2. What are the causes?
  3. What are the complications in this type of disease?
Answers:
  1. Diabetes mellitus (Type I).
