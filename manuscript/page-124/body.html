CEREBELLAR FUNCTION TESTS FINGER NOSE TEST

  1. Make the subject comfortably seated in a chair
  2. Explain the test procedure
  3. Ask the subject to touch the nose with the tip of the forefinger and then reach out to touch your finger tip held just within the subject arm’s reach
  4. Ask the subject to repeat the movement between nose and target finger as quickly as possible
