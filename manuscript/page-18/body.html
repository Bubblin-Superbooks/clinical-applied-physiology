Case no. 2:

Brown Sequard Syndrome

(Hemisection of spinal cord)

A 21 year old soldier returns from the war with a gunshot wound in his spinal column. Upon examination, you notice that he has weakness of left lower extremity.

In addition he has loss of pain and temperature perception on the right side from about the level of his navel downward, vibration joint position sense and discriminatory function are reduced in the left leg
