Case no. 7

Myasthenia Gravis

A 68 years old man was brought to a neurology clinic with symptoms of tremors of hand.

He suffered from profound weakness of muscles, gets easily fatigued, more so in the evening. It was noticed that his eyelids were drooping and the infrequently blinked his eyes.

The patient tended to speak in a low, faint voice.


Questions:
  1. What do you think is the possible diagnosis?
  2. What is the cause and mention the changes in neurotransmitters.
  3. How can it be treated?
