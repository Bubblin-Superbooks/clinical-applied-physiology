A 65 year old man presented in the medicine OPD with complaints of tremor. He has a stooped posture, festinating gait and loss of associated movements, masked face and slurred voice.

Upon examination muscle tone showed cogwheel rigidity

Questions:

  1. Name the disease the patient is suffering from?
  2. What are the sites of lesion?
  3. Mention the changes in the neurotransmitter in this disease.

Answers:

  • Parkinson disease
  • Basal ganglia
  • Decrease in Dopamine/ Increase in Acetylcholine
  • Reduction in choline acetyl transferase in nucleus basalis
